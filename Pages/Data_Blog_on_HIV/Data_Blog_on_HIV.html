A Brief Description:

This project explores global patterns in the outbreak of HIV/AIDS through data-driven analysis. It raises awareness about the prevalence and impact of HIV/AIDS by examining real-world data, highlighting regional trends, and contributing to public discourse on this critical health issue. The project showcases the use of advanced data science techniques, including data scraping, cleaning, visualization, and machine learning, to tackle real-world health challenges. Through this work, I gained valuable experience in data science, predictive modeling, and health analytics, further enriching my portfolio of health-related data projects.
